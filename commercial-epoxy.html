Commercial Epoxy FAQ

How much does commercial epoxy flooring cost?

Commercial epoxy flooring costs $4-12 per square foot depending on the system type, surface condition, and project size. High-traffic areas requiring thicker coatings cost more than light-traffic spaces. We provide detailed commercial proposals with line-item pricing and ROI analysis.

How long does commercial installation take?

Most commercial projects take 2-4 days including surface prep, coating application, and curing time. We can work nights and weekends to minimize business disruption. Larger projects may take 1-2 weeks depending on square footage.

Will epoxy hold up to heavy foot traffic?

Yes! Commercial epoxy systems are specifically designed for high-traffic environments. They resist wear from thousands of daily footsteps, shopping carts, and equipment. Many retail stores and offices use epoxy specifically for its traffic durability.

Is epoxy flooring slip-resistant for commercial use?

We add slip-resistant additives to all commercial floors to meet OSHA safety standards and reduce liability. The texture level can be customized based on your needs - from subtle grip to aggressive traction for wet areas.

Can you match our company colors or add our logo?

Absolutely! We can match any company color using custom tints and integrate logos, striping, or directional markings directly into the floor. This creates cohesive branding throughout your space.

Do you provide commercial warranties?

Yes, all commercial installations include written warranties covering material defects and workmanship. Warranty length varies by coating system (typically 5-15 years) and is detailed in your proposal.
