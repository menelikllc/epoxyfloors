About Menelik Epoxy Floor Coating

Connecticut's Trusted Partner for Professional Epoxy Flooring Solutions

Our Story

Menelik Epoxy Floor Coating, LLC was founded with a simple mission: to bring professional-grade epoxy flooring solutions to Connecticut homeowners and businesses at honest, transparent pricing. What started as a commitment to quality craftsmanship has grown into Connecticut's trusted name in epoxy floor coatings.

We recognized that Connecticut property owners deserved better than the substandard garage floor paints and DIY kits available at big box stores. They needed a professional contractor who understood the science of concrete preparation, chemical bonding, and long-lasting protective coatings. That's exactly what we deliver on every project.

Today, we serve residential, commercial, and industrial clients throughout Connecticut, from single-car garages to 50,000+ square foot warehouses. Every installation is treated with the same attention to detail, backed by our licensed expertise and lifetime support guarantee.
