Decorative Epoxy FAQ

How much do metallic epoxy floors cost?

Metallic epoxy floors cost $8-15 per square foot in Connecticut depending on the metallic effects, number of colors, and complexity. While more expensive than standard epoxy, the dramatic visual impact makes them worth the investment for showrooms and upscale homes.

Can I choose any color I want?

Yes! We can match virtually any color using custom tinting. Bring us paint chips, fabric swatches, or color codes and we'll create the perfect match for your space. We can also create multi-color designs and patterns.

Will metallic floors look the same as photos?

Each metallic floor is unique - that's part of their beauty! While we can achieve similar effects to photos, exact replication isn't possible due to the organic nature of metallic application. We can provide small test areas or mockups if desired.

Are decorative floors as durable as standard epoxy?

Absolutely! Decorative epoxy floors have the same durability as standard systems. They're sealed with the same protective top coats and can handle residential or commercial traffic depending on the system specified.

Can you add my company logo to the floor?

Yes! We can integrate company logos, custom graphics, or decorative borders directly into your epoxy floor. This is popular for showrooms, corporate offices, retail stores, and restaurants wanting cohesive branding.

What's the most popular decorative finish?

Flake systems are the most popular for garages and workshops due to their texture, variety, and ability to hide imperfections. Metallic finishes are most popular for showrooms, basements, and upscale residential spaces where visual impact is priority.
